Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience to enrol?

Most courses have beginner tracks — no prior coding or design experience needed. Our admissions team will assess your background and recommend the right starting point.

Are courses fully online?

Most programmes run online-first with optional in-person sessions in Cape Town, Johannesburg, and Nairobi. Cohort live sessions are recorded for later review.

What is the job guarantee policy?

If you complete the programme and don't find relevant employment within 6 months, you attend the next cohort free of charge. Full details are in the enrolment agreement.

Are payment plans available?

Yes — we offer monthly instalments, income-share agreements for qualifying students, and corporate invoicing for employer-sponsored enrolments.
