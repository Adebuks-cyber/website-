Who We Are

A Different Kind of Tech School

Digital Skills Training Academy was born out of frustration — frustration with outdated curricula, passive lecture halls, and degrees that cost a fortune but didn't reflect what the industry needed.

We started with six students, two instructors, and a rented co-working space. Today we operate across three cities, with a fully online programme that reaches students on six continents.

Our instructors are active professionals — developers, data engineers, and designers who bring live industry context into every class. We update our curricula every quarter to match the market.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To provide accessible, industry-aligned digital skills training that transforms learners into confident professionals — regardless of their starting point, background, or geography.

We measure success not by enrolment numbers, but by employment outcomes. Every programme is designed with a single question: does this get students hired?

🔭 Our Vision

A world where the digital divide is a thing of the past — where anyone with drive and curiosity can access the knowledge they need to build a meaningful career in technology.

By 2030, we aim to have trained over 100,000 professionals and established learning partnerships in 50 countries.

💎 Core Values

  • ✅ Outcomes over credentials
  • ✅ Radical transparency
  • ✅ Continuous curriculum updates
  • ✅ Inclusive, supportive community
  • ✅ Industry partnership first

🤝 Our Promise

If you complete our programme and don't land a relevant job within 6 months, we'll give you another cohort free of charge — no fine print.

We only win when you win. That's not marketing; that's our business model.

How We Got Here

Our Journey

From a small co-working room to a global training platform — each step shaped by student feedback and industry partnership.

2015

Founded in Cape Town

Six students, two instructors, one mission: practical digital skills that actually get you hired.

2017

Expanded to Johannesburg & Nairobi

Opened two new campuses after graduating our first 500 students with a 91% placement rate.

2019

Launched Online Platform

Moved 40% of content online, reaching students across Africa, Europe, and North America.

2021

10,000 Graduates Milestone

Celebrated our 10,000th graduate and launched our first employer hiring network.

2024

AI & Cloud Programmes Added

Responded to market demand with new tracks in AI/ML engineering and cloud architecture.
